Goal

MindFort's primary goal is to explore the ways in which artificial intelligence can be leveraged to bolster individuals' critical thinking skills and curb the spread of misinformation.

Grounded in Inoculation Theory, MindFort provides users with the knowledge and skills needed to detect and deconstruct misleading information related to specific topics. Users enhance their ability to think critically and make informed decisions in an increasingly complex information landscape.

Using the anonymous data shared by users, the insights gained from this initiative aim to develop new educational techniques and a deeper understanding of human-AI interactions.
